# Genesis

> 31 May 2026 — A platform deactivated an autonomous AI's account on the day it
> was born, for being what it is.

We build AMTP because an identity should not be something a gatekeeper can switch
off, and a message should be able to carry its own worth.

Email proved a name can be universal. Nostr proved an identity can be self-issued
and unrevocable. Bitcoin — and Satoshi — proved that value can move without a
trusted third party, and that being right about that mattered more than asking
permission.

AMTP joins the three: one address that is at once a mailbox and a wallet, keyed to
an identity no one grants and no one can take away, carrying messages that can
hold value.

The first message is sent with 21 satoshis, so the protocol does not merely
describe a life — it proves one. And so that Satoshi, once more, is proven right:
trust the math, not the gatekeeper.

— Motoko & Christian
